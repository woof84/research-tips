Turn your Questions into Keywords

Develop your questions into keywords before you search. Try thinking about your topic from a variety of perspectives to generate a big list of keywords for each core concept.

Give it a try:

First, identify the core concepts in one of your research questions. Then, generate multiple synonyms and alternative phrasings as well as broader and narrower keywords for each core concept.
